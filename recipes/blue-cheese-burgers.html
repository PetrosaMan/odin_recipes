Blue Cheese Burgers

Recipe

Blue cheese burgers aren't just basic hamburgers — they're a real treat, and the wise cook will make up a dozen or so for the freezer.

If you like blue cheese, you'll never forget these burgers.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced fresh chives
  • 4 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt/li>
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • ¼ teaspoon hot pepper sauce
  • 12 hamburger buns, split

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef, chives, blue cheese, salt, Worcestershire sauce, pepper, mustard, and hot pepper sauce together in a large bowl until well combined.
  2. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours.
  3. When ready to cook, preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Remove beef mixture from the refrigerator; form into 12 patties.
  5. Cook patties on the preheated grill until an instant-read thermometer inserted into the center reads at least 160 degrees F (71 degrees C), about 5 minutes per side.
  6. Serve on hamburger buns.
